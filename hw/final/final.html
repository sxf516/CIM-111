How to Pack

  • PACK LIGHT!
  • Everything is super casual, so no need for fancy club attire
  • A lot of activewear becuase you will be spending your days exploring
  • Pack layers because it can be freezing in the morning and night, but very hot during the day
  • Do NOT bring a blowdryer, the American ones blow the fuse
  • No such thing as too many bathing suits!

Where to Study: University of New South Wales

Loopholes to Making Your Schedule:

  • Plan classes from Tuesday through Thursday
  • Lectures are not mandatory
  • Online classes are great because you do not meet in person
  • Art classes on the Paddington campus are very difficult (avoid if you can!)

Classes to Take:

  • World Religions
  • Marine Biology
  • Australian Legends
  • Indigenous Australia

Classes NOT to Take:

  • Terror and Religion
  • Life of Performance
  • Understanding Human Rights
  • Art
